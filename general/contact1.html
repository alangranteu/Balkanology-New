Contact Me



You are welcome to contact me with feedback about the site, corrections, suggestions about other sites I should link to, questions about your travel plans, updates based on your own travels, or extravagant praise. Especially the last one. I try to reply to all messages, but I maintain this site in my spare time so sometimes it takes a while. Please note that I am NOT a travel agent - I cannot book flight tickets for you or arrange your visa, and I don't have government or business contacts in any Balkan country.

Please include a meaningful subject to help me to distinguish your message from spam. Apologies for not having a clickable link to my e-mail or a form through which you can contact me from this page - both methods produced too much junk mail.
