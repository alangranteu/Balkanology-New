European Turkey | Links

I haven't made any attempt to provide a comprehensive set of links for Turkey - the Turkey Travel Planner should allow you to find any further information you need. Many of the more general sites listed in the Overview of the Balkans are also useful for planning a trip to Turkey.

Travel and Transport

  • Turkey Welcomes You

    The official website of the Turkish Culture and Tourism Office.

  • Turkey Travel Planner

    The experienced travel writer Tom Brosnahan has produced an excellent site with a wealth of reading. If you are planning a trip to Turkey that extends beyond a few days in Istanbul, this site will repay careful study. A particularly nice touch is the explanation of "how to decipher the TCDD web site".

  • TimeOut Istanbul

    Information about culture, nightlife, eating out, and sightseeing in Istanbul.

  • TCDD - Turkish Railways

    Timestables for trains within Turkey - not easy to navigate if you can't read Turkish, but help is at hand: see the next link. There is also a useful page with details of international trains to Europe, including sample fares to a range of destinations.

  • Varan
    Ulusoy

    Two of Turkey's best-known bus companies, with a wide range of both international and domestic services.

  • IETT

    Information about Istanbul's array of public transport options.

  • Turkish Airlines
    Ataturk Airport (Istanbul)
