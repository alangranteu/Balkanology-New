Inland Croatia

Although Croatia's coast attracts most of the attention its contrasting inland regions have plenty to see, ranging from the Central European architecture of Zagreb and Varaždin to the tranquil beauty of Plitvice Lakes, and activities such as hiking in the Samobor hills or birdwatching in Slavonia.
